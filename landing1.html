srmvmun'16

Bridging Differences Marshalling Diplomacy

5thOct - 6thOct'16

CONFERENCE DETAILS

SRM Vadapalani MUN is the introductory Model United Nations of the college which encompasses 3 councils namely United Nations Security Council(UNSC), United Nations General Assembly (UNGA), and United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) which are headed by some of the most experienced and accomplished board members from diverse origins across the country.SRM Vadapalani is all set to redefine diplomacy by hosting its first ever Model United Nations conference from 19th August 2016 to 21st August 2016! More than 400 delegates from all over the country are expected to conjunct in this event which would upkeep diplomacy and healthy debating and translate into an opportunity for creating a plethora of possible solutions to the various obstacles faced by humanity in a wide array of affairs ranging from Geo-political to Socio-economic and would also bring about the best in delegates by molding them into more adept leaders for tomorrow.

Secretary

Yash

As this year’s Secretary General, it is my distinct pleasure to welcome you to the Intra edition of SRMVMUN 2016, the Model United Nations Conference hosted by SRM University-Vadapalani.I am honored to have the opportunity to serve as the Secretary General of SRMVMUN 2016, and with my team I would try my best to give you one of the best experience and a bunch of everlasting memories. The hard work of the organising committee has brought to life a event, which unfurls in all its grandeur on 5th October 2016. One of the best Executive Board, Heated up debates, Dynamic agendas and Conventional Committees.As the world suffers in the dark provinces of terror and apathy, we aim to inculcate awareness in times of ignorance. To build leaders of tomorrow who solve the crises of today. Every person attending the conference will have widened horizons, expanded knowledge and intrigued intellects. We nurture young minds in an international experience to forge a brave youth, confident in itself, and assured enough to lead the world forward. Join the brightest minds from the country and the world as they join hands in standing against all that plagues our world and solve it. As the famous quote says,"The supreme art of war is to subdue the enemy without fighting." With this hope of moving from ignorance to awareness, from apathy to sympathy, from darkness to light, I welcome you all to SRMVMUN 2016.

Yash Khairnar
Secretary General
